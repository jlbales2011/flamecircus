Frequently Asked Questions

Are you insured?

Yes! We have a 2 million dollar policy through Specialty Insurance Agency. All our performers are insured.

What type of events do you do?

Weddings, private parties, childrens parties, festivals, grand openings, corporate functions and everything in between.

How do I hire you?

We have a "Book Now" button on our home page, which will redirect to a booking service. You may also contact us directly via email at info@flamecircus.com to discuss details.

Do you travel?

Yes we do! Anything outside our local area will include travel expenses.

When is deposit and payment due?

Deposit is required at the time of booking and is nonrefundable. Payment is due one day prior to the event and may be paid via TheBash, PayPal, or Venmo. Cash is also accepted.

More Questions?
