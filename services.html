Fire


A fire performance is an electrifying blend of skill, danger, and artistry that captivates audiences of all ages. Our shows feature various fire props, such as dragon staff, poi, sunwheel, and juggling torches, each adding unique flair. We also offer specialized acts like fire eating, where flames are expertly controlled to create stunning visual effects. Whether you're seeking a high-energy spectacle or an intimate, mesmerizing show, we guarantee an unforgettable experience that will leave your guests talking long after the flames have died down.

Basic Fire Show: A choreographed fire performance featuring a dynamic show with a variety of fire props. Perfect for those seeking an exciting visual experience at an entry-level price.

Standard Fire Show with Intermision: Includes a choreographed fire performance featuring fire eating, fire bubbles, and a captivating 8-ring optical illusion intermission. Our most popular package.

Premium Fire Show: Our top-tier package includes a full choreographed fire performance with fire eating and fire bubbles, intermision, plus a fire bubble photo op and social media advertisement to promote your event.

Add-Ons to Any Package: Enhance your event with a fire bubble photo op, juggling class (7 participants) for more guest interaction, or social media advertisement to spread the word about your experience.

Juggling


We offer choreographed juggling performances that are expertly crafted, featuring impressive tricks and synchronized routines that showcase precision and creativity. Our LED clubs are a must-see! For a more interactive experience, our roaming jugglers provide ambient entertainment, mingling with guests and performing close-up feats that add a lively, engaging atmosphere to any event.
