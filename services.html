Services

Fire


A fire performance is an electrifying display of skill, danger, and artistry that captivates audiences of all ages. Our shows can feature a variety of fire props, including poi, dragon staff, sunwheel, juggling torches, and more, each adding a unique flair to the performance. We also offer specialized acts like fire eating, where flames are expertly controlled and manipulated to create stunning visual effects. Safety is our top priority, which is why all our performances are attended by a trained assistant who ensures that all fire safety protocols are strictly followed. Whether you're looking for a high-energy spectacle or a mesmerizing, intimate show, our fire performances promise an unforgettable experience that will leave your guests talking long after the flames have died down.

Standard Fire Show: Two 10-15 minute sets. Includes multiple fire props, fire eating, juggling, and a special surpise for the grand finale.

A La Carte: Choreographed fire eating with fire bubbles. Ambient options and individual fire sets also available.

Juggling


We offer choreographed juggling performances that are expertly crafted to music, featuring impressive tricks and synchronized routines that showcase precision and creativity. For a more interactive experience, our roaming jugglers provide ambient entertainment, mingling with guests and performing close-up feats that add a lively, engaging atmosphere to any event. The more jugglers, the more fun we can even incorporate juggling passes and collaborative tricks with multiple entertainers, elevating the excitement and creating unforgettable moments. Whether it's a solo performance or a group act, our juggling services promise to bring a unique and memorable flair to your event.

Coreographed Performance: Planned routine to preselected song

Ambient/Roaming Performance (LED and Day Props): Hourly with breaks. Two 15-20 minute sets per hour.

Optical Illusions


Experience the mind-boggling magic of "8 Rings," our most unique optical illusion performance. Watch as circles seem to float effortlessly in space, creating mesmerizing patterns that captivate and astound. This visually stunning act is a true spectacle, leaving audiences both amazed and perplexed. Seeing it live is an unforgettable experience, offering a special kind of magic that must be witnessed to be believed.

Coreographed Performance: Planned routine to preselected song

Ambient/Roaming Performance: Hourly with breaks. Two 15 minute sets per hour. Additional sets available.

Stilts
